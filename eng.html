About us

We are a company specializing in surface treatment of different materials. Our greatest experiences is in metal surface treatment such as sandblasting, turning, welding followed by anticorrosion treatment such as zincging and wet paint spray painting. Besides painting metal parts we also wet paint spray paint MDF-parts according to RAL and NCS color samplers. We offer an unique process of renovating aluminim alloys. This process is based on mechanical and chemical treatmet and it is a great and cost effective solution when it comes to renovating of your classic car, motorbike, machine aluminum parts. In this fields we offer only the best quality services and are able to deliver your finished products right to your doorsteps.

Spray Painting

Wet paint spray painting is a benchmark in field of surface treatment. Besides painting metal parts we also wet paint spray paint MDF-parts according to RAL and NCS color samplers.




It is an affordable process with a superb long-lasting effects.

Sandblasting

Universality, great price to quality ratio are the main reasons why sandblasting became one of the most popular surface treatment procedure. Thanks to its versatility it can be applied on many different materials such as metals(constructions, welds, pillars, barrier railing, classic cars, rims...), wood(wooden constructions, furniture, chairs, tables, antiques), glass, bricks, concrete, walls, precast units, cellars and plastics. Sandblasting mainly degreases, evens the surface and it prepares the material for further surface treatment e.g. wet paint spray painting or metalization.




Furthermore sandblasting can be used as a final finish in procedures such as glass frosting and others. Thanks to its high effectiveness and affordability, sandblasting gained its application in technical, artistic and other fields.

Chemical polishing

Step by step we tried out various techniques to restore the former shine of our aluminum alloys. Thanks to the combination of mechanical and chemical procedure we were finally able to achieve the desired finish. Why should you chemically polish Aluminum? The parts will look like new. Older imperfect alloys with typical structure named as „Orange crust“ will keep their appearance. Newer alloys will have an even surface with natural shine and silver colour. This process will prevent parts from getting greasy and dirty. The shine is long-lasting and easy to maintain. Almost all of the alloys of different sizes can be polished.




Advantages:
  • Improved corrosion restitance and durability
  • Reduced friction and wear
  • Optimum reflectivity
  • Attractive appearance


Disadvantages: Our technology can not eliminate deep scratches and gaps that usually occur on mild alloys. Therefore we advise you to first grind this imperfections.
